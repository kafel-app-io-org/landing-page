Your Web3 Wallet for Seamless Transfers & Giving

Experience the next generation of digital finance with our blockchain powered wallet. Send, receive, deposit, withdraw, and donate securely onchain and offchain. Leveraging smart contracts for transparency and automation, and NFC offline payment technology for instant transfers without internet connectivity, we ensure your financial life is secure, efficient, and always accessible.

We Deliver Best Solution

Kafel is a blockchain-based platform built to solve urgent cash and donation distribution problems. It enables donors to support verified campaigns via a mobile App. In addition, users benefit from an offline wallet feature: tokens are issued to users when online, digitally signed, stored locally, and can be exchanged offline. This reduces dependence on physical cash which is often damaged, counterfeit, or refused by merchants and compensates for a crippled banking infrastructure.

Beneficiaries may claim or spend aid via multiple channels: stablecoins like USDT, QR-code vouchers, NFC-enabled cards, etc., ensuring inclusivity even without smartphones or steady internet.

Kafel fuses blockchain’s immutability, diversified redemption channels, and offline transaction logic to bring secure, transparent, and resilient aid distribution to vulnerable communities.

Transfer Funds Instantly with NFC

Send money to friends, family, or merchants securely via NFC-powered offline payments. Transactions are recorded on-chain once reconnected, ensuring both speed and transparency no internet required.

Purchase Goods & Services

Use your wallet balance to pay merchants directly through smart contract–secured transfers. Whether online or offline, every purchase is fast, verifiable, and tamper-proof.

Your Donations in Your Hands

Stay in full control of your giving. As a donor, you can decide when, where, and how your contributions are allocated. Blockchain-backed smart contracts provide proof that your funds are reaching the intended cause.

Track & Verify Your Donations

Gain full visibility into your impact. Follow your donations in real time with transparent, auditable records, ensuring accountability and building trust between you and beneficiaries.
